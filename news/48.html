Television in Georgia

Source: Wikipedia

Television in Georgia was introduced in 1956, when Georgia was still known as the Georgian SSR. The most watched TV channel is TV Imedi which is known for its pro-government orientation, followed by 1 pro-opposition TV channel: TVPirveli
